Manjari Asawa, Ph.D.

Manjari Asawa is a seasoned engineering professional with 22 years of experience in solution architecture, development, operation, product management, technical due-diligence and strategic planning.

She provides a depth of perspective drawing from her unique experience spanning different parts of the ecosystem, from service operators (Dish Networks, Verizon, AT&T), network equipment providers (Cisco, Qualcomm, HPE, Nominum), and solution integrators (Wipro).

She has a unique ability to innovate and architect solutions that encompass multiple technologies in order to address business and technical needs in emerging technology areas such as 5G networks, edge architectures, data analytics, IoT and micro-grids.

She has a proven track record of making successful strategic decisions, managing multimillion dollar products and services from inception to launch under tight deadlines, and successfully leading mid-sized cross-domain teams
